MÁDARA is the name of a unique Northern wild herb (Galium Verum) that only grows in a pure soil, free from pesticides and other chemicals. Picked as a symbol of all organic Northern ingredients used in brand's products, it is also a popular woman’s name in Latvia. Founded in 2006 and nestled along the Baltic Sea, AS MADARA Cosmetics is an organic cosmetics manufacturer, whose formulations are based on highly active natural ingredients, harvested in the meadows, lakes and forests of Baltic/Northern region. Both ingredients and final formulations are certified according to the international ECOCERT/Cosmos standard.

Nature is generous source of beauty ingredients.

“From revitalizing birch water to omega-bursting berry seeds, aromatic herbs and even mushrooms. Using cutting-edge technology, we refine nature’s raw materials into biologically active substances, and work them into clean formulas that deliver proven results“
